About Me

I'm a Computer Vision & ML Engineer with an M.Eng in Engineering Geomatics from the University of Calgary, with peer-reviewed publications in navigation, visual odometry, and UAV systems.

My work sits at the intersection of aerial robotics, embedded hardware, and real-time perception — building systems that let drones see, reason, and navigate autonomously, from indoor GNSS-denied environments to wide-area methane detection.

Currently at Draganfly Inc. leading a Raspberry Pi → NVIDIA Jetson migration and implementing obstacle avoidance with ROS 2 and PX4. In parallel, I consult for Aerometrix Ltd. on UAV-based methane detection.
